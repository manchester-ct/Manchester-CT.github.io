Quantitative Monoidal Algebra

Calum Hughes

In this seminar I will talk about joint work with Wojciech Różowski, Ralph Sarkis, and Fabio Zanasi, where we developed a monoidal version of quantitative algebraic theories. Then, I will also discuss some work in progress (with Fabio Zanasi) regarding distributive laws between quantitative monoidal theories.
